中文介紹

龍山神料企業是一家深耕二十年的香品專賣老字號，專注於高品質神料、香品、祭祀用品的販售與推廣。我們堅持「顧客至上，銳意進取」的經營理念，持續為廣大顧客提供專業、真誠且快速的服務。

從傳統廟宇供奉用香，到居家日常淨化、祝禱所需的香品，龍山均秉持精選原料、品質為先的原則，贏得廣大顧客信賴與喜愛。

我們不僅實體經營穩固，也積極拓展線上通路，歡迎透過 Shopee、Lazada、Facebook 與我們聯繫、選購。

English Introduction

Longsan Enterprise is a well-established incense and ritual goods company with over 20 years of experience. We specialize in offering high-quality incense, temple offerings, and spiritual supplies, proudly upholding our core values: “Customer First, Progress with Purpose.”

From temple incense for religious rituals to daily spiritual needs at home, our products are selected with care and commitment to quality, earning the trust and support of thousands of loyal customers.

In addition to a solid physical presence, we are also actively expanding online through Shopee, Lazada, and Facebook. You're welcome to connect with us and explore our offerings.
