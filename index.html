企業介紹 Company Introduction

龍山神料企業是一家專業經營香品與神料用品的老字號，擁有超過二十年的豐富經驗。我們致力於提供各式各樣的香品、祭祀用品、神像神桌等高品質商品，以滿足不同信仰與文化需求的顧客。

本公司秉持「顧客至上、誠信經營、品質第一」的經營理念，深耕本地市場，並持續拓展線上銷售平台，為顧客帶來便利與信賴的購物體驗。

Longsanenterprise is a well-established incense and religious item supplier with over 20 years of business experience. We specialize in a wide range of traditional incense products, deity statues, altar accessories, and ceremonial supplies to meet the spiritual and cultural needs of our customers.

We uphold the business principles of “Customer First, Integrity, and Quality Priority,” serving both walk-in customers and online shoppers with sincerity and professionalism.
